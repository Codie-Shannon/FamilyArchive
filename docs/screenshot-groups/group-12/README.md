# Family Archive Group 12 Evidence

Group 12 adds reviewable structured historical dates and explicit
source-collection provenance without changing preserved files.

## Delivered capability

- Exact, year-only, decade-only, approximate and unknown date representations
- Independent structured confidence and accepted/suggestion review state
- Required date source and reasoning notes
- Immutable date before/after revision evidence
- Stable collection, physical album, box and envelope records
- Stable scan-batch records
- Multiple reviewed source links per approved photo
- Immutable provenance attachment and removal revisions
- Owner-only management and access

## Preservation boundary

Group 12 changes reviewed database facts only. It does not write, move, rename,
replace or delete original, derivative or quarantine files. Embedded EXIF dates
remain suggestions until reviewed. No storage path, hash or original-download
surface was added.

## Fictional proof boundary

All screenshots use the isolated, idempotent `Group12DemoSeeder` dataset. The
photo, source collection, scan batch, caption, date and New Zealand setting are
fully synthetic. No real family identity, face, media or source record appears.
