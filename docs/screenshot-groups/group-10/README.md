# Family Archive Group 10 Evidence

Group 10 delivers the first owner-only private archive browsing experience.

The gallery displays only approved photo records through authenticated thumbnail derivatives. The detail view displays only authenticated web-display derivatives. Missing and failed derivative states remain safe and never fall back to original pixels.

## Preservation boundaries proved

- Authenticated Archive Owner access only.
- Approved photos only.
- Private thumbnail derivatives in the gallery.
- Private web-display derivatives on detail pages.
- Stable archive identity and deterministic pagination.
- No original filename, storage path, hash, intake identifier or original-pixel route.
- No download, edit, delete, replace, approve, share, regenerate, select or bulk-action controls.
- Missing and failed derivative states fail safely.
- Browsing is read-only and does not generate or mutate archive data.

All records and images shown in this evidence set are sanitized fictional demonstration data.
